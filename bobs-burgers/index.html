Bob Belcher

  • Age: 46
  • Favorite Food: Burgers
  • Favorite Thing: Puns

Robert "Bob" Belcher is the owner and chef of Bob's Burgers.
Bob grew up working with his father, "Big Bob", in their family restaurant.
He is a down to earth and relatable character when compared to most cartoon fathers.
While sometimes passive, he does his best to be a good father to his 3 children.

Linda Belcher

  • Age: 44
  • Favorite Food: Italian
  • Favorite Thing: Her Family

Linda Belcher is the wife of Bob Belcher.
She is very supportive of her husband and kids in all of their endeavors.
She is much more enthusiastic and wild than her counterpart Bob.
She tends to get ahead of herself sometimes, but owns up to her mistakes in the end.

Tina Belcher

  • Age: 13
  • Favorite Food: Buns
  • Favorite Thing: Horses and Butts

Tina Belcher is the eldest daughter of the Belcher family.
She is an angsty teen that tends to be very awkward and obsessive.
She loves horses, zombies, and butts above all else.
She is very protective of her younger siblings.

Gene Belcher

  • Age: 11
  • Favorite Food: Everything, especially Cheese
  • Favorite Thing: All things music

Gene Belcher is the middle child of the Belcher family.
He is a goofy child that is very creative.
His favorite sound is farts and he will make sure you know it.
He is very afraid of snakes.

Louise Belcher

  • Age: 9
  • Favorite Food: Candy
  • Favorite Thing: Kuchi Kopi

Louise Belcher is the youngest daughter of the Belcher family.
She is mischievous and always up to something.
Bob is her favorite parent.
She tends to be the one looking out for siblings, despite them being older than she is.
